What runs here — today, on a live network

Burn-only issuance

No premine, no block reward, no issuer. Every unit is Bitcoin provably destroyed — 1 burned sat = 1 sat, verified by SPV in consensus.

The programmability Bitcoin refuses

CTV, CSFS, OP_CAT, CSV and recursive covenants — running for real, not debated on a mailing list.

Bitcoin facts in consensus

Contracts settle against real Bitcoin transactions: a payment confirmed on Bitcoin unlocks funds here. No oracle, no federation, no custodian.

Private cash

Shielded payments (Sapling) between entry and exit — final in about a minute, invisible in between.

BTC ─ burn ▸ private payments ▸ Settlement Provider ─ BTC

Bitcoin sees two transactions. BATHRON executes an entire economy in between — and settles it back to native BTC through an open market of providers.

Status: experimental. A private testnet is running the full loop today. Public testnet, documentation and a bug bounty are next — if you build on Bitcoin and want early access, write to us.
